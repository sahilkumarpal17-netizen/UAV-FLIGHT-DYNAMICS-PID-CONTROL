## PID Tuning Summary

| Configuration | Kp | Ki | Kd | Settle Time | Overshoot | SS Error |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| High Kp (Oscillation) | 8.0 | 0.1 | 0.5 | 0.60 s | 8.19 m | 0.27 m |
| Low Kp (Slow Response) | 0.5 | 0.1 | 0.5 | 2.05 s | 6.89 m | 1.64 m |
| High Ki (Overshoot) | 2.0 | 3.0 | 0.5 | 0.95 s | 54.69 m | 10.0 m |
| **Optimal (Stable Hover)** | **2.0** | **0.1** | **1.0** | **1.05 s** | **4.99 m** | **0.01 m** |

### Key Observations

- **High Kp** → Fast but oscillating. Drone bounces around the setpoint.
- **Low Kp** → Slow, sluggish response. Drone never fully reaches target.
- **High Ki** → Integral wind-up causes 5× overshoot (drone flies to 54 m!).
- **Optimal** → Clean, stable hover. Steady-state error < 1 cm after ~1 s.

## Flight Dynamics Recap

| Motion | Axis | Motor Control |
|--------|------|--------------|
| Roll | X (longitudinal) | Left vs Right motor speed difference |
| Pitch | Y (lateral) | Front vs Back motor speed difference |
| Yaw | Z (vertical) | Diagonal motor torque difference (CW vs CCW) |
| Throttle | Z translation | All four motors equally |

---

## PID Formula

```
thrust(t) = Kp·e(t) + Ki·∫e(t)dt + Kd·de(t)/dt + m·g
```

Where:
- `e(t)` = target_height − current_height
- `m·g` = gravity feed-forward (hover compensation)
